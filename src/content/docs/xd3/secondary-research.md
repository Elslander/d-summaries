| Primary Research                                                                                                       | Secondary Research                                                                                 |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **First hand**, you do the research                                                                                    | **Second hand**, someone else did the research                                                     |
| Understand what is it really like. Dive into the facts and feelings of people. Find your own conclusions and insights. | Check assumptions, prep primary research. Explain and support what your found in primary research. |

Secondary research is the process of **searching for, review** and drawing transferable **learnings** from prior research findings or achieval informaiton.

You seek out **work by others** and use their insights to support your own ideas and conclusions.

## Sources for secondary researh

### Web search

- Keywords
- Open-ended
- New resources
- Starting point
- Operators search syntax

### Forums & Social media

- Social listening (reviews, comments,...)
- Authentic experiences
- Users discuss their interests, challenges, frustrations and workarounds.
- X, Reddit

### AI chat platform

- Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT, Perplexity,...
- NoteLM

**Warning**:

- AI chatbots like ChatGPT and Gemini spread false or misleading information in 1 in 3 cases, according to US research by NewsGuard.
- Despite a year of technological progress, AI systems are failing nearly twice as often at their basic tasks: distinguisging facts from lies.

**AI lies**: The evolution of incorrect information in 1 year of different chatbots is apparent.

**Not a resource**: For research purposes information that you can not track back to its source is not usable! The source should be double checked and validated by you!

**NoteLM**: A solution to limiting incorrect facts.

### Whitepapers, reports & scientific magazines

- Research, data and commentary on specific topics.
- Authored by business, governments, or nonprodit organisations.
- White papers are mostly paid.
  - Pew Research Center
  - Forrester
  - Harvard Business Review
  - Statisa
  - Nature
  - Science

### Academic research papers

- Research reviewed by experts.
- Very credible sources.
- Literature with existing related research.

| White paper                                                                            | Academic paper                                                   |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Research done in functio of product or marketing. Often paid (so watch out for biases) | Peer reviewed, and fact checked. Should be neutral / subjective. |
